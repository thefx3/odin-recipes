The Cantonese Rice

The Cantonese Rice is a very basic but very savorous Vietnamese Meal.

Composed of rice, peas, scrumbled eggs, ham and onions, it has everything for a healthy diet and very easy to make.

I will show you how !

Time preparation : 20 min

Ingredients :

  • Rice basmatic 2 cups
  • Ham 150gr
  • 4 eggs
  • Leeks 100gr (optional)
  • Carots 100gr (optional)
  • Peas 100gr
  • 1 Onions
  • Coriander
  • Soja sauce

Process

  1. Add the rice in a pot with boiling water
  2. Cut the ham, onion, and other vegetables (carrots, leeks, onions) in small cubes
  3. Mix the 4 eggs in a bowl, add pepper and salt and put it in a oiled pan
  4. Stir it to make scrumbled eggs then take them out of the pan. Replace them with the sliced vegetables.
  5. When the rice is cooked, rince it and let it dry as much as possible
  6. Put it in a pan with oil at medium fire to fry it. Wait until it get crispy
  7. Once the vegetables are well cooked and the rice crispy, add the vegetables and the ham, and the eggs
  8. Finish the recipe by adding soja sauce on the rice, small pieces of coriander and any other condiments you might want
  9. Stir from time to time so the rice don't get stucked to the pan and is not burned
  10. Add oil on the whole mix if needed
  11. Serve it hot and...
  12. Enjoy !
