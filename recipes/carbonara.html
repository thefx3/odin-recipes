Carbonara

The Spaghetti Carbonara is an italian meal. Very popular among the italian but in Europe as well, I will present you an easy recipe to make one.

Composed of fresh pasta, pancetta (bacon), eggs and parmiggianno, you will only love it !

Time preparation : 20 min

Ingredients :

  • Fresh Pasta 500gr
  • 4 eggs : 2 wholes and 2 yolks
  • Parmeggianno/li>
  • Pancetta 200gr
  • Salt and Pepper

Process

  1. Add the fresh pasta in a pot of boiling water
  2. Cut the pancetta and put it a pan with oil
  3. Wait until it's gold and turn down the fire
  4. In the meantime, put 2 wholes eggs and 2 yolks in a bowl. Stirr it and add salt and pepper
  5. Take out 2 big spoons of the water from the fresh pasta and add it in the bowl of eggs
  6. Add the parmeggianno in it as well and mix the whole. It should become a thick cream
  7. Take out the pancetta when it's cooked and leave the greass in it.
  8. Take out the fresh pasta out of the water, rince it with water and put it in the greased pan
  9. Small fire for the pasta, and it should fries it and when pasta are fried enough, add the cream you made
  10. Turn off the fire and it's ready to taste !
  11. Enjoy !
