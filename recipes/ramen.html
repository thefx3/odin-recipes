Ramen

The Ramen is a delicious japanese meal. Quite savorous, I will present you an easy recipe to make one.

Composed of various vegetables, wheat noodles, spices and herbs with leaves of meat.

Time preparation : 30 min

Ingredients :

  • Wheat noodles 500gr
  • Pork or Beef 200gr
  • 4 eggs
  • Leeks 100gr
  • 2 carrots
  • Chinese cabbage 100gr
  • Coriander
  • Soja sauce

Process

  1. Add the wheat noodle inside the pot and add just enough water to cover them
  2. Cut the beef/pork in thin slices
  3. Cut all the vegetables in small pieces (coriander, carrots, leeks and cabbage)
  4. Put some oil a pan, heat it up and add the sliced vegetables for a few minutes (slow fire)
  5. In a small bowl, mixed the 4 whole eggs, and add them on a separate pan with oil
  6. Stir it regularly to do scrambled eggs then turn down the fire
  7. Once the noodles are soft and al dente slow down the fire and add more water if there is no more
  8. Take out the noodles with the water in a bowl, add some spicy sauce and soja and a bit of coriander
  9. Take out the vegetables and mix them with the noodle
  10. Add the scrambles eggs
  11. Add any spices or condiment you want (salt, pepper, parsley)
  12. Enjoy !
