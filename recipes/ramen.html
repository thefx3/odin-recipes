The Ramen is a delicious japanese meal. Quite savorous, this is an easy recipe on how to make one.

Composed of various vegetables, wheat noodles, spices and herbs with leaves of meat.

Time preparation : 30 min

Ingredients :

  • Wheat noodles 500gr
  • Pork or Beef 200gr
  • 4 eggs
  • Leeks 100gr
  • 2 carrots
  • Chinese cabbage 100gr
  • Coriander
  • Soja sauce
